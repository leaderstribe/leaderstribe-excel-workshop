# Excel for Data Analysis for Women
### A Leaders Tribe Facilitator Site

Welcome, facilitator! This site is your complete guide to running the **Excel for Data Analysis for Women** workshop — an 8-week, two-level training built for women in African community organisations, NGOs, cooperatives, savings groups, and small businesses.

This is **not** a general information site. Everything here exists to help you, the facilitator, **stand in front of a room (or a Zoom call) and teach Excel with confidence.**

---

## Who this training is for

Learners are everyday women who use Excel (or want to start) for real work: tracking savings group contributions, recording market sales, managing NGO beneficiary lists, or reporting to donors and cooperative leaders. Most Level 1 learners have **never used Excel before** — some have never used a laptop for work before. Go slow, go kind, go practical.

## How to use this site as a facilitator

1. Read the [Setup page](setup.md) at least one week before the workshop starts.
2. Read the Level Overview before you teach that level.
3. For each session, read the full session page the night before — it has everything: script, activity, dataset, mistakes to expect, and homework.
4. Use the [Facilitator Tips](facilitator-tips.md) and [Common Errors](common-errors.md) pages as your "cheat sheet" during class.
